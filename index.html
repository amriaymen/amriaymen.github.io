FAQs

  • Why choose Sketch n'go over a full-time designer?

    A full-time senior-level designer costs over $100,000 per year, not to mention benefits. With the monthly subscription plan, you can pause and resume as needed to pay only for the work available.

  • Is there a limit to the number of design requests?

    No limit! You can add as many requests to your queue as needed, and they will be completed one by one.

  • How long does it take to receive designs?

    Most requests are completed in two days or less, while complex requests may take longer.

  • Who are the designers?

    Sketch n'go is run by a single founder, and direct clients will work with them. For power-up requests like animations or custom illustrations, partner designers will be used.

  • Which programs are used for design?

    Figma, Adobe Suite, and Blender are commonly used for design requests.

  • How can I request designs?

    Clients can request designs using Trello in a variety of ways, including direct requests, shared Google docs or wireframes, or recorded Loom videos. If it can be linked to or shared in Trello, it can be used.

  • What if I don't like the design?

    No problem! The design will be revised until you're 100% satisfied.

  • Can I use the service for a single request?

    Yes! You can pause your subscription after a single request and return when you have more needs, even if it's years later.

  • Is there a refund policy?

    Due to the high quality of work, there will be no -outneed for refunds.
